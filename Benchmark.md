#A collection of process models for testing structuring techniques.

# Benchmark #

Please help us to extend our benchmark by sharing your process models with us. Preferably, models should be created using [Oryx](http://oryx-project.org/backend/poem/repository) "BPMN 2.0 Processes" stencilset, but we appreciate your participation regardless of serialization formats. The main request from us - models should have non-trivial structure. If you decide to use [Oryx](http://oryx-project.org/backend/poem/repository), please follow our modeling guidelines: Your process model should be composed of tasks, AND (parallel) gateways, and XOR (data-based exclusive) gateways. The process model must have a single source and a single sink task. Every node of the model must be on a path from the source to the sink. Every task must have at most one incoming and at most one outgoing arc. Every gateway must be a split or a join. All nodes must be labeled.
